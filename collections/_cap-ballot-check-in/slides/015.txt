---

After the voter marks their ballot, the voter:

Inserts their marked ballot into the B envelope 
Seals the B envelope 
Completes the information requested on the outside of the B envelope
Signs the B envelope and has a witness sign as well

In completing the B envelope, the voter is verifying three critical pieces of information:  

- their identity 
- that they are a registered voter residing in Fairfax County 
- that they are authorized to vote absentee in the current election
